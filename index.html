EDUCATION AND CARE FOR UNDERPRIVILEGED CHILDREN


The mission of the Butterfly Effect Foundation is to improve the lives of children in need by meeting their basic requirements and ensuring that they receive an education. Our goal is to close the educational gap for such children and encourage them so they can thrive and realise their full potential.

ARTISTS FOR ARTISTS (AFA)


The Artists for Artists Initiative, led by the Butterfly Effect Foundation, aims to provide support and assistance to young and talented artists who may be encountering financial or other obstacles in their artistic careers. By organizing art events featuring renowned eminent artists and educators, this initiative seeks to generate funds and create opportunities for artists in need.

ANIMAL WELFARE


The Butterfly Effect Foundation works relentlessly to promote animal rights, enlighten local communities, and effect positive change. Our dedicated team is passionate about making a world free from animal cruelty and where animals live in secure, loving environments.

PROMOTION OF CULTURAL AND REGIONAL PERFORMING ARTS


Our foundation's goal is to recognise and highlight the value of cultural and local performing arts. In order to give artists a platform to shine and to promote a greater understanding and appreciation of their art forms, we would like to engage closely with artists, communities, and cultural institutions.
