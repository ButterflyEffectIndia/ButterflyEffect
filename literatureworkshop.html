Promotion and encouragement of writers and poets

We aim to showcase the richness of regional and indigenous literature, fostering a deeper appreciation for their unique perspectives, stories, and cultural heritage. Our aim is to promote and encourage the talents of regional and indigenous writers and poets who often go unnoticed and underappreciated.

We want to enable these creative individuals to reach a larger audience and be recognised for their contributions to the literary world through workshops, literary events and opportunities.

By promoting regional and indigenous literature, we aim to preserve cultural diversity and encourage dialogue, understanding, and appreciation among diverse communities.

Our mission is to celebrate these literary voices and create a literary landscape that reflects the richness and diversity of our country.
